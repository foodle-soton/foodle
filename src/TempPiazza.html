The Piazza is the largest food court area in the Highfield Campus. A recent refurbishment has transformed the Piazza into a modern restaurant and Student Social Centre. You are welcome to use your Catered Student Card in the main Piazza area during lunch service.

It is located next to the Student Union and has the capacity for 300 seated guests on two levels. There is also a small outside patio area that seats approximately 30 to 40 people. The restaurant offers a varied choice of drinks, cooked meals and take away meals.

At the Piazza you can enjoy a full English breakfast each morning, whilst at lunchtime we offer a variety of hot dishes which are all served with vegetables, potatoes and there is a well stocked salad bar.

A jacket potato bar offers hot and cold fillings. For the socially and healthy conscious we offer a good selection of Fair-trade products sold along with Gluten free, low fat cakes and vegetarian dishes.

We continue to offer healthier options with the introduction of our new Souper Soups, available in the Food on the Go area of Piazza, together with a large variety of sandwiches and snacks.
